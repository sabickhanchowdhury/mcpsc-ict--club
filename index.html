About Us

More Than Just a Club

MCPSC ICT Club is a hub for technology enthusiasts at Mirpur Cantonment Public School & College. We believe technology is the language of the future — and we're here to help you speak it fluently.

From web development and programming to robotics, cybersecurity, and AI — our club is where curiosity turns into creation. Join a community of thinkers, builders, and digital innovators.

Departments

Each department drives a unique aspect of our club's mission — find where you belong.

🎨
Creative & Design

📢
Media & Publication
Managing social media, newsletters, and content to share our journey and inspire the wider community.
🔐
Cybersecurity
